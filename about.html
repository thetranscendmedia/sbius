About SBI Investigation Bureau

Understanding Our Mission and Organization

Our Mission

The State Bureau of Investigation (SBI) is dedicated to investigating federal crimes, protecting national security, and serving the public through professional law enforcement. Our mission is to uphold the Constitution and enforce federal laws with integrity and professionalism.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the premier federal law enforcement agency, known for excellence in investigation, innovation in forensics, and unwavering commitment to justice. We strive to be a trusted partner with state and local agencies in combating crime and protecting our communities.

Core Values

🛡️ Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of ethical conduct and honesty in all our operations.

⚖️ Justice

We pursue fairness and accountability in every investigation and prosecution.

💪 Excellence

We strive for the highest level of professionalism and competence in our work.

🤝 Collaboration

We work with partners locally, nationally, and internationally to achieve our goals.
