Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report criminal activity?

You can report tips by calling 1-800-TIPS-911, submitting our online form, or visiting any of our regional offices. All tips are taken seriously and investigated appropriately.

How long does it take to get a response?

For general inquiries, we typically respond within 48 hours. For tip reports, initial triage occurs immediately, and follow-up investigations begin right away.

Can I remain anonymous when reporting a tip?

Yes, we accept anonymous tips. You can use our secure anonymous tip line at 1-800-TIPS-911 or submit information through our secure online form.

How do I submit a FOIA request?

FOIA requests can be submitted online through our website, by mail to our headquarters, or by email to foia@sbi.gov. Please allow 20-30 business days for processing.

Who should I contact about media inquiries?

Please contact our Public Affairs Office at media@sbi.gov or call 202-555-0150. They handle all media requests and press inquiries.

How can I apply for a job with SBI?

Visit our Careers page to view current openings and submit your application online. You can also call our HR department at 202-555-0120.
